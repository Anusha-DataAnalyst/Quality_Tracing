# VisiOn Platform — Requirements

FR-01: The platform shall provide user-friendly interfaces for its users.
FR-02: The platform shall support access to the citizen's information to authorized users only.
FR-03: The platform shall analyse the users' authorizations based on their needs to perform their activities.
FR-04: The platform shall control the usage of its services to authorised users only.
FR-05: The platform shall identify when a citizen's consent for using its information is required.
FR-06: The platform shall analyse whether a citizen's information is made available to authorized users.
FR-07: The platform shall analyse whether citizen's information is modified only by authorized users.
FR-08: The platform shall analyse whether citizen's information is provided only to users who need them.
FR-09: The platform shall provide the result(s) of the privacy analysis to PA users in different formats (diagrams, tables, models, etc.).
FR-10: The platform shall provide PA users with interfaces to visualize information regarding citizen input in different formats.
FR-11: The platform shall provide citizen users with an interface to manage their privacy requirements.
FR-12: The platform shall provide citizen users with an interface to provide different kinds of consents concerning their personal information.
FR-13: The platform shall provide a trust/distrust analysis to ensure that authorized users will not misuse their granted authorities.
FR-14: The platform shall support PA users to perform information flow analysis to identify potential security/privacy breaches.
FR-15: The platform should be equipped with manuals (user guide, documentation) specialized for both PA and citizen roles.
FR-16: The platform shall provide citizen users with interfaces to visualize their privacy requirements in different formats.
FR-17: The platform shall guarantee the availability of its services.
FR-18: The platform shall support citizens in raising awareness about the importance of protecting their privacy.
FR-19: The platform shall notify citizens about the benefits/risks of signing/not signing a consent and ask them to confirm their choice.
FR-20: The platform shall support the analysis of different threats that might endanger the citizen's privacy requirements.
FR-21: The platform shall provide an interface for citizen users through which they can state their privacy requirements.
FR-22: The platform shall support an analysis of the infrastructure of systems used for citizen's information transmission.
FR-23: The platform shall identify whether information is subject to a citizen's privacy requirement.
FR-24: The platform shall be easy to install, configure and use.
FR-25: The platform shall assist citizens with suggestions that facilitate the privacy requirements definition process.
FR-26: The platform shall assist its users to protect their granted credentials.
FR-27: The platform shall identify all users involved in each citizen's information transmission.
FR-28: The platform shall provide PA users with information about the infrastructure of systems used for information transmission.
FR-29: The platform shall support the analysis of whether citizen's privacy requirements are consistent with the social and organizational aspects of the system.
FR-30: The platform shall enable the PA user to assess the trustworthiness of its services.
FR-31: The platform shall identify all users that are allowed to modify a citizen's information.
FR-32: The platform shall identify the legal owner(s) of information.
FR-33: The platform shall provide citizen users with an interface to know who may use their information, when and for which purposes.
FR-34: The platform shall support an analysis to specify whether the infrastructure used for citizen's information transmission is compliant with citizen's privacy requirements.
FR-35: The platform shall provide analysis of whether citizen's information is transferred through secure channels/means.
FR-36: The platform shall support the automated analysis of citizen's privacy requirements.
FR-37: The platform shall support a consistency analysis that considers the different privacy requirements of the citizen.
FR-38: The platform shall provide different analysis results in acceptable time to PA users.
FR-39: The platform shall support a threat analysis that enables detecting any weakness/vulnerability in the system.
FR-40: The platform shall support a trust/distrust analysis among users to analyse whether they will behave as expected.
FR-41: The platform shall support the elicitation of citizen's security requirements for ensuring the privacy of its information.
FR-42: The platform shall support the PA user to approve the privacy requirements specified by the citizen.
FR-43: The platform shall support the PA to analyse the completeness of the privacy requirements defined by the citizen.
FR-44: The platform shall enable the PA user to notify/inform the citizen if further information is required to verify privacy requirements.
FR-45: The platform shall analyse the infrastructure of the socio-technical system whenever external actors are involved.
FR-46: The platform shall represent/capture obligations as specified by domain experts and derived by applicable law governing personal information transmission.
FR-47: The platform shall be able to analyse whether specified information transmission is compliant with related laws.
FR-48: The platform shall visually notify the PA user if information transmission meets/does not meet privacy requirements and applicable laws.
FR-49: The platform shall provide PA users with an integrated interface through which they can perform all their activities.
FR-50: The platform shall provide citizen users with a web-based interface through which they can perform all their activities.
FR-51: The platform shall represent the PLA concerning citizen information to PA users as a summary of allowed/forbidden activities.
FR-52: The platform shall support the PA user to verify whether privacy requirements defined by the citizen are consistent with their PLA.
FR-53: The platform shall notify the PA user when an activity is conflicting with the PLA specified by the citizen user.
FR-54: The platform shall enable citizen users to read and sign any consent related to the usage of their private information.
FR-55: The platform shall enable the PA user to specify how the citizen's consent was obtained in case it was not directly obtained by the platform.
FR-56: The platform shall be able to provide its services to users on different operating platforms and devices.
FR-57: The platform shall provide dedicated notification means for citizens with special needs.
FR-58: The platform shall support an analysis of any hardware and software that uses/facilitates the use of the citizen's information.
FR-59: The platform shall support an analysis to enable PA users to visualize whether citizen information is used only for the purposes it has been provided for.
FR-60: The platform shall support analysis of whether the PA user can use the citizen's information for a specific activity.
FR-61: The platform shall support information integrity analysis.


FR-62: The platform shall support an integrated analysis of security/privacy threats taking into consideration the social and organizational aspects of the system.
FR-63: The platform shall support the identification of citizen's privacy requirements violations at run-time.
FR-64: The platform shall support the notification of citizens about any violation to their privacy requirements.
FR-65: The platform shall help citizen users in specifying their security requirements by providing suggestions and/or hints.
FR-66: The platform shall allow citizen users to manage the privacy of their composite information separately.
FR-67: The platform shall provide an interface for citizen users through which they can verify their privacy/security requirements.
FR-68: The platform shall ensure that only legally allowed users can access its functionalities.
FR-69: The platform shall identify who are the performers of the activities that use the citizen's information.
FR-70: The platform shall support identifying the potential system weaknesses/vulnerabilities that can be used to threaten citizen's privacy requirements.
FR-71: The platform shall guarantee the consistency of its information used by both PA and citizen users across time and space.
FR-72: The platform shall guarantee the validity of any information used for providing any of its services.
FR-73: The platform services shall be scalable and provide good performance independently of the number of users.
FR-74: The platform shall support an analysis to identify whether a user shall be part of an information transmission.
FR-75: The platform shall support the analysis of information aggregation that might lead to privacy breaches.
FR-76: The platform shall support authority/permission delegation analysis among its users.
FR-77: The platform shall keep records about any change to citizen's information, who performed the change and when.
FR-78: The platform shall identify all the users of the citizen's information.
FR-79: The platform shall support an analysis that identifies whether permissions are granted only by authorized users.
FR-80: The platform shall identify the information needs of each activity/task of the system.
FR-81: The platform shall provide suggestions to PA and citizen users to assure privacy requirements are satisfied.
FR-82: The platform shall help in specifying the consent type required from the citizen based on the intended usage of its information.
FR-83: The platform shall be accompanied with training sessions for both PA and citizen users.
FR-84: The platform shall notify the PA user by mail (on request) if citizens' privacy requirements are violated.
FR-85: The platform shall notify the PA user about any violation to citizen privacy requirements by different means as defined by the PA user.
FR-86: The platform shall support a trust/distrust analysis for capturing expected behavior of actors of the system.


FR-87: The platform shall capture the specialization/generalization relationships among the different roles played by users of the system.
FR-88: The platform shall support an analysis to identify whether information storage mechanisms are compliant with citizen's privacy requirements.
FR-89: The platform shall support a separation of duties analysis so that a user cannot play roles whose simultaneous adoption can compromise citizen privacy.
FR-90: The platform shall enable the PA user to define how information can be composed/decomposed and specify the relation between composite information and its sub-items.
FR-91: The platform shall inform citizen users about social engineering attacks.
FR-92: The platform shall enable citizen users involved in an information transaction to identify all other users involved in such transaction.
